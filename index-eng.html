GENERAL INFORMATION

A geoglyph is a geometric or figurative pattern more than 4 meters long, engraved on the ground. Some geoglyphs are so large that they can only be seen from the sky. There are several types of construction of geoglyphs. One of them is to dig and put a marking on a certain depth from the surface of the Earth. And the second is to lay special gravel or large stones and make an image. Another type - planting trees with the image of certain patterns.
Mysterious geoglyphs of Turgai - relief geometric and figurative patterns on the surface of the Earth, visible from the height of the flight of birds, are known all over the world. About 200 such figures are known on the territory of Kostanay region. Information about the objects was first obtained at the general meeting of the Orenburg Scientific Archival Commission in 1909. In 2007 they were discovered by Dmitry Dey during the study of space images in Google Earth. Scientific research was carried out by employees of the archeological laboratory of Kostanay University under the direction of Andrei Logvin. According to the assumptions of archaeologists, the geoglyphs had a ritual purpose.
