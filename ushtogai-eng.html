Ushtogai square

The mysterious Turgai geoglyphs are currently attracting the attention of scientists all over the world. The largest of them, the isosceles Ushtogai square. Its area is 11 soccer fields, that is 8 hectares.

It is located in Amangelda district, 15 kilometers east of Ushtogai village, and is a geometric figure with marked diagonals. The length of the quadrilateral is 287 meters and the diagonal is 406 meters. It consists of one hundred and one embankments with a diameter of 10-12 meters and a height of one meter.
